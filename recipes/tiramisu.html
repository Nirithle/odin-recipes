Tiramisu recipe

Description

Tiramisu is an elegant and rich layered Italian dessert

made with delicate ladyfinger cookies, espresso or instant espresso,

mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, Marsala wine, rum and cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Mix creamy filling
  2. Dip lady fingers. Add the espresso and liqueur
  3. Layer mascarpone
  4. Repeat
