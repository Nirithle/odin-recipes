Pizza recipe

Description

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish typically

consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough

topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients,

baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Mozzarella
  • Oregano
  • Tomato sauce
  • Flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the base
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Top and bake
